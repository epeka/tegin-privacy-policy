Your Privacy,
Our Commitment

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Tegin is a marketplace and community app for sharing items. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have. By using Tegin, you agree to the practices described here.

01

Data We Collect

  • Account data — email address and user ID created upon registration.
  • Profile data — display name, city, and profile photo (only if you choose to provide one).
  • User content — listings, wishes, chat messages, and photos you upload to the app.
  • Technical data — app diagnostics, crash logs, and basic device and app metadata to help us maintain reliability.
02

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display listings, wishes, and facilitate chat between users.
  • To ensure safety, support moderation, and prevent abuse on the platform.
  • To maintain and improve the reliability and performance of the app.
03

Sharing & Third Parties

Your data is stored and processed using trusted service providers, including Firebase (Google Cloud), which provides our backend infrastructure. These providers act on our behalf and are bound by appropriate data protection agreements.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or any third parties, ever.

04

Data Retention

We retain your account and content data for as long as your account is active, or for as long as necessary for legal and safety reasons. Once you request deletion, your account and associated data will be removed in accordance with applicable law.

05

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can edit your profile fields and update or remove uploaded content at any time within the app.
  • You can revoke camera or photo permissions granted to Tegin through your device's system settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at any time (see contact section below).
06

Children's Privacy

Tegin is not directed to children under the age of 13, or the applicable minimum age in your local jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Tegin after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
